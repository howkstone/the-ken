Features

Voice-first by default
Voice is the primary way to interact - no buttons required. Say who you want to call, and The Ken does the rest.
Video calling & video voicemails
Tapping a contact's face opens their history - video voicemails they've left, recent calls, and a bold call button. Family can leave short video messages that play automatically, so the connection stays alive between calls.
Large photo contact tiles
Faces and names fill the screen - no numbers, no menus. Contacts are set up and managed remotely by family or carers.
Medication & appointment reminders
Scheduled alerts with voice announcement and a visible on-screen prompt - no dismissal required. Family or carers can add anything remotely: prescriptions, appointments, birthdays, bin day.
Clear stereo speaker
Loud, warm and calibrated for speech intelligibility, not music. Strong enough to hear clearly from an armchair across the room.
Hearing aid compatible
Pairs directly with modern Bluetooth hearing aids, so calls and audio stream through them automatically. For many users, this will be the single most important feature on the device.
High-contrast touchscreen
Large interface elements, bold typography and strong contrast throughout. Designed from day one for reduced vision - not retrofitted as an accessibility setting.
Nightlight mode
Dims automatically to a warm, low glow at a set time each evening. Enough light to orient in the dark without disturbing sleep.
Adjustable stand
Solid, weighted base with smooth angle adjustment - bedside table, kitchen counter, armchair tray. One hand is enough; no tools, no fiddly mechanisms.
Daily photo carousel
Family send photos from their phones and they appear on screen throughout the day - grandchildren, dogs, a view from a walk. The device feels alive even when no one calls.
Physical reset button
Hold for 10 seconds and the device returns to a clean working state. No tech support call, no family member driving over.
Remote family access
Every setting, contact, photo and calendar entry is manageable remotely by family. The device is designed so the user never needs to configure or troubleshoot anything - but they can if they want to.
Connects to headphones, keyboards & more
Optional accessories - wireless headphones, a large-key keyboard, a remote control - pair automatically with no setup. Home WiFi connects once during installation and is never touched again.
Annual feedback programme · £20/year
Subscription pricing is just £20 per year, covering ongoing support and development. We refund that year's fee to the first 5 people whose feedback we go on to implement - hearing from real users is how The Ken gets better.
Over-the-air software updates
Improvements install automatically overnight. The user will never notice it happening.
