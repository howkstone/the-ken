## PRODUCT POSITIONING (from theken.uk/friends preview)

**What it is:**
- Video calling device for elderly users
- Simplified, voice-first interface
- 10.1" / 13.3" / 15.6" touchscreen options
- £275 base price + £4.99/month subscription

**What it's NOT:**
- Smart home device
- Tablet replacement
- General-purpose computer
- "Technology for seniors" (condescending framing)

**Key features highlighted:**
- Touchscreen (portrait orientation)
- Medication reminders
- Daily photo carousel
- Nightlight mode
- Calendar & appointments
- Adjustable stand
- Standard stereo speaker (baseline)

**Optional upgrades:**
- Larger displays (13.3", 15.6")
- Wide-angle camera (1080p, 120°)
- Battery backup (2hr or 4hr)
- Premium speakers

**Accessories:**
- Protective case (£30)
- Emergency pendant (£20)
- Headphones (£25)
- Keyboard & mouse (£15)
- Wall mount (£15)
- Remote control (£15)

## TARGET AUDIENCE (from theken.uk/friends)

**Primary buyer:** Adult children (30-60 years old)
- Managing parent's care
- Lives remotely from parent
- Concerned about isolation
- Tech-savvy themselves
- Budget: £200-400 for peace of mind

**End user:** Elderly parent/relative (70+ years old)
- Limited tech experience
- May have dexterity/vision issues
- Values simplicity over features
- Wants to stay connected with family
- May be skeptical of new technology

**Dual-audience messaging:**
- To buyer: "Peace of mind, always connected"
- To user: "Simple to use, just works"
- NOT: "Technology for the elderly" (patronizing)

## CRITICAL BRAND RULES

1. **Never condescend** - "simple" not "simplified for you"
2. **Function over flash** - utility beats aesthetics
3. **Honest pricing** - no hidden fees, clear value
4. **Warm neutrals only** - cream/gold, never red/green/purple
5. **Serif for emotion** - Cormorant for warmth, Jost for clarity
6. **Large, forgiving UI** - touch targets 280px+, 22px+ fonts
7. **No smart home language** - "video calling device" not "smart display"
8. **Respect both audiences** - buyer needs peace of mind, user needs simplicity

## COMPETITIVE DIFFERENTIATION (implied from positioning)

**vs GrandPad:** Lower subscription (£4.99 vs £60/month)
**vs Komp:** Video calling included (not just photos)
**vs Echo Show:** Privacy-first, no ads, elderly-optimized
**vs iPad:** Single-purpose, no learning curve, family-managed

**Key advantage:** Purpose-built for one thing (video calling), not general-purpose device disguised as elderly tech.
